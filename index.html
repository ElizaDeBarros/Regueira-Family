Manoel Nicolao Regueira Pinto de Souza

09/01/1829 - 09/24/1908

The legitimate son of Antonio Germando Rigueira Pinto de Souza and Anna Eustaquia Rigueira Pereira de Bastos, Manoel belonged to one of the most notable families in the State of Pernambuco, Brazil. He was born in September 1st, 1908. Manoel graduated from the Academy of Olinda. He was first married to Mrs. Maria Honorata Regueira de Bastos with whom he had three children: Francisca Regueira, Idalina Regueira, and Victoriano Regueira Pinto de Souza. His second wife was the esteemed Mrs. Candida Brandão Regueira. Dr. Manoel Nicolau was a major in the National Guard, a Knight of the Order of Christ, and held various public positions, including that of fiscal of the State Treasury. Manoel passed away at his residency on September 24th, 1902.



Victoriano Regueira Pinto de Souza

11/02/1864 - 06/20/1937

Dr. Victoriano Regueira Pinto de Souza was born on November 2, 1864, in the then-town of Serinhaém, Pernambuco, Brazil. He graduated from the Recife Law School and practiced law in Recife until 1892 when he was appointed municipal judge of Brejo da Madre de Deus. In January 1893, he was appointed cabinet officer to the then-governor Dr. Barbosa Lima, a position he left after being named public prosecutor of Ipojuca. He was later transferred to São Lourenço da Mata. Some time afterward, he was appointed judge of Cabrobó and, in 1900, was reassigned to Triunfo. Finally, he moved to Recife, where he opened a law office. Dr. Victoriano Regueira was married to the esteemed Mrs. Maria Anna de Lyra Regueira, with whom he had ten children: Adherbal de Lyra Regueira, João Álvares Pereira de Lyra Regueira, Manoel Nicolau de Lyra Regueira, Antônio Luiz de Lyra Regueira, Maria Celina Regueira Gondim, Maria de Lourdes Regueira Bello, Maria do Carmo Regueira Medeiros, Maria da Conceição Regueira Medeiros (wife of Mr. Oswaldo Medeiros), Maria Luiza Regueira da Costa e Silva, and Maria Adelaide Regueira Ramos. Dr. Victoriano passed away on June 20, 1937.
